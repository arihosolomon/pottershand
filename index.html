About PHF

A Mission Rooted in Faith and Service

Potters Hand Foundation (PHF) Uganda is a faith based Not for Profit organization which was founded in January 2024 by Charity Kyomugisha-Nuwagaba who draws inspiration from her personal journey to finding purpose and a strong desire to see women and young people use their God given gifts and placements to create lasting impact for generations. She is is deeply committed to helping women and young people discover, understand, and pursue their potential—enabling them to contribute meaningfully to social and economic development.

PHF focuses on a holistic model of empowerment that integrates training in leadership and economic skills, health promotion, and the promotion of social cohesion and community building, especially among women and youth.

Contact Us to Join Our Mission

Through awareness-raising, capacity-building, and community-based programs, PHF is committed to transforming lives by equipping individuals not only to thrive individually, but to become agents of change within their families, communities, and the nation at large.

Vision

Purpose – driven leaders. Transformed Communities. Lasting social and economic impact.

Mission

To nurture purpose driven women and young people who positively transform their families, communities and economies.

Core Values

Values That Shape Our Work

At Potters Hand Foundation, our values guide every decision we make and every program we implement.

Integrity: We uphold high standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions, ensuring that our work reflects strong ethical and moral principles.

Compassion: We serve with empathy, humility, and a genuine commitment to uplifting and supporting the well-being of individuals and communities.

Empowerment: We believe in unlocking the potential within every person by nurturing purpose, building skills, and creating opportunities for individuals to lead meaningful and productive lives.

Service: Guided by faith and a heart for humanity, we dedicate ourselves to serving others and contributing to sustainable social and community transformation.

Our Commmitment

Leadership Development: We aim to build confident, values and purpose-driven leaders who actively participate in community and national development

Skills Development: empower women and youth with practical skills, entrepreneurship capacity, and technological and financial knowledge to build sustainable livelihoods and economic independence.

Social cohesion and community building: foster strong, cohesive, and resilient communities by promoting dialogue, collective action, intergenerational learning, and cultural connection.

Health Promotion and well being: promote holistic well-being by increasing community awareness, strengthening access to health and mental health services, and encouraging healthy lifestyles

Counselling and spiritual support: nurture the emotional well being and resilience and promote spiritual growth.

Call to Action
